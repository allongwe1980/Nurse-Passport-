About

One verification.
A career without borders.

Nurse Passport exists to close the gap between the nurses the world has trained and the patients who need them — without exploitation, opaque fees, or months of paperwork.

The problem we set out to fix

The world is short millions of nurses while skilled, qualified nurses in lower-income countries struggle to cross borders. The path is gated by fragmented councils, repeated verifications, expensive intermediaries, and a process that treats the nurse as the last priority.

We rebuilt that path around the nurse. Verify credentials once — at the primary source — and carry a portable, trusted record into every corridor you qualify for.

What we believe

Our principles

🛡️

Ethical by default

WHO Safeguards compliance is built in. We never place nurses through corridors that strip source health systems.

💸

Nurses never pay

Verification and placement are free for nurses. The people doing the work keep what they earn.

🔐

The nurse owns the data

A tiered consent model means a nurse decides exactly what each recruiter can see, and when.

🌍

Built for underserved markets

We start where the need is greatest, designing for the realities of the corridors others ignore.
